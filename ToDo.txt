Beispiele für :g / :v
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
	:g/.*/m0
		This will reverse the order of the lines in the current file.
		m0 is the ex command to move the line to line 0.
	:v/./.,/./-1join
		Replaces multiple blank lines with just one blank line.
